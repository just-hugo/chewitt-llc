Nice to meet you.

I design, build, and host websites for artists, small business owners, and practitioners of the helping professions — such as therapists, grief counselors, or psychiatrists — who want help reaching potential clients online.

Before focusing on web design, I was a software engineer and IT professional with over 15 years experience in a diverse range of industries, including data analytics, real estate, and the arts.

I'm the first in my family to graduate college, where I earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in Theatre. I received all my technical training from on-the-job experience working for my parents, who owned a computer repair shop and internet service provider in the early 90s.

Website solutions with a personal touch.
