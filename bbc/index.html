Our Services

ABA Therapy

Applied behavior analysis is an evidence-based mode of treatment with a variety of applications. This personalized treatment approach has been proven to improve social skills, communication, and academic performance.

Homeschool consultation

Get expert consultation on homeschooling, including scheduling, curriculum, and tailored learning to the needs of each child, including those with special needs and learning difficulties.

School consultation

We work closely with educators to identify challenges and develop strategies for addressing them using evidenced-based practices and principles from ABA.

BCBA Supervision

Our BCBA Supervision services provide support and guidance to practitioners seeking to become Board Certified Behavior Analysts (BCBAs).

Advocacy

We provide parents with support in navigating the school system to ensure your children are receiving the support they need. Get individualized support tailored to your child and situation.

Disclaimer

Applied Behavior Analysis informs all services offered; however, our services may also provide additional strategies that are not specific to ABA practice.

About

At Beyond Behavior Consulting, we are dedicated to providing individualized services that are tailored to the specific needs and goals of each client.

We believe that the most effective approaches to behavior changes are those that are based on a thorough understanding of each client’s unique challenges and strengths.

We're committed to working closely with clients and their families to develop personalized treatment plans that support their success.

We are excited to bring our knowledge, skills and experience to work with clients to help them reach their goals.

Staff

Laurice Seely M.S., BCBA

Clinical Director & Owner

  • Master's of Science, Psychology
  • Board Certified Behavior Analyst
  • 15 years of ABA experience

Laurice has been in the field of Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA) since 2009. Her experience in ABA has been diverse, ranging from in-home direct services, working as a Family Teacher in a group home, teaching in an ABA preschool classroom, and serving as a School Based Behavior Specialist.

She received her Bachelor’s of Science Degree in Psychology from University of Phoenix, went on to earn her Master’s of Science degree in School Psychology with a Pupil Personnel Services (PPS) credential from National University. Laurice also completed a Graduate Certificate in Applied Behavior Analysis from Brandman University, and passed the Behavior Analyst Certification Board (BACB) exam to become a Board Certified Behavior Analyst (BCBA).

She currently lives on the beautiful Central Coast of California with her husband, son, and their three pets. When not working or homeschooling her son, Laurice enjoys spending time in nature, especially at the beach.

Laurice is passionate about providing services to a wide array of individuals in need. She is excited to bring her knowledge and skills to help clients reach their goals!
